Haraka Afya

A cross-platform health application developed with Flutter that provides quick access to medical services, health information, and emergency contacts. The app serves both Android and iOS users with a single codebase.

Key Features:

  • Real-time doctor consultation booking
  • Medicine reminder system with notifications
  • Emergency SOS with location sharing
  • Health articles and symptom checker
  • Multi-language support
  • Offline functionality for critical features

Technologies Used:

Flutter, Dart, Firebase, Google Maps API, BLoC pattern, Hive for local storage

Impact:

The app has helped thousands of users access healthcare services quickly, especially in remote areas with limited medical facilities.

Shopfinity (Available on Play Store)

An e-commerce platform specifically designed for bookshops to manage sales, inventory, and bulk orders efficiently. The system includes analytics dashboards for tracking sales performance and customer behavior.

Key Features:

  • Inventory management with barcode scanning
  • Multi-user access with role-based permissions
  • Bulk order processing system
  • Sales analytics and reporting
  • Customer management and loyalty programs
  • Integration with payment gateways

Technologies Used:

Java, Firebase, Android SDK, Google Books API, ML Kit (for barcode scanning), Stripe API

Impact:

Shopfinity has been adopted by over 50 bookshops, helping them increase operational efficiency by 40% and improve sales tracking accuracy.
